CIVIL ENGINEERING

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways

The objective of the B.Tech graduate program in Civil Engineering is to give the understudies great comprehension of civil engineering arrangements in a global, societal and ecological setting, steady with the standards of sustainable improvement. The course will make the understudies capable Identifying, planning and taking care of civil engineering problems that meet indicated execution, cost, time, well being and other quality needs and objectives with proficient and moral responsibility.The department also offers two PG programs, M. Tech. in Structural Engineering and M.Tech. in Construction Technology under full time category.

Labs and Computing Description
Surveying Laboratory

The planning and design of all Civil Engineering projects such as construction of highways, bridges, tunnels, dams etc. are based upon surveying measurements. Moreover, during execution, project of any magnitude is constructed along the lines and points established by surveying. Thus, surveying is a basic requirement for all Civil Engineering projects. .

Concrete Technology Laboratory

oncrete technology guide provides information on materials, properties, mix design, types, strength and durability of concrete. The concrete technology laboratory equipped with high end instruments such as Universal testing machine, compression testing machine, cement autoclave testing machine, vibrating table, slump test apparatus, Electric Concrete Mixer, L box & U Box for testing self compacting concrete etc. deliver the students a high value practical experience.

Highway Engineering Laboratory

Highway engineering is an engineering discipline branching from civil engineering that involves the planning, design, construction, operation, and maintenance of roads, bridges, and tunnels to ensure safe and effective transportation of people and goods. Highway engineers must take into account future traffic flows, design of highway intersections/interchanges, geometric alignment and design, highway pavement materials and design, structural design of pavement thickness, and pavement maintenance.
Environmental Engineering Laboratory

Department has developed “Environmental Engineering Laboratory” as “Environmental Audit Cell” which is well equipped with the instruments used for measurement of different parameters of Ambient Air, Stack Emission, Waste Water, Portable Water and Noise. Environment Audit Cell under the department of civil engineering is providing Environmental Management System (EMS) adequacy certificate as per the need of industries.
