COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Computer engineering combines the fields of electrical engineering and computer science. A computer engineer is involved in both the design of computer architecture and the design of hardware and software.

It is a four years (eight semester) full-time undergraduate program. Out of eight semesters, seven semesters are dedicated to in house study involving course work and projects. Final semester is dedicated to Industrial Project. Students spend entire eighth semester in an IT company and do a project.The curriculum is designed by keeping industrial and research needs, as well as the rapid development in the IT industry. Flexibility in curriculum is provided through Electives, Seminars and Projects. The program is supported by well-equipped laboratories consisting of the state-of-the-art servers & computers.There are well qualified and experienced academic staffs. Almost all of them are actively involved in research. This creates a lively and stimulating atmosphere to study Computer Engineering. The Faculty members publish their research work in national and international journals and conferences. Students routinely present Technical Papers at National Level Symposiums, and achieve laurels.To give more and more industrial exposure to students, the department has strong industry interface with leading companies like

  • IBM
  • CISCO
  • INFOSYS
  • ORACLE
  • NVIDIA
  • MICROSOFT
. This helps to ensure that department’s research and teaching is well informed and relevant to the needs of society.

Labs and Computing Description
Apple – iOS Laboratory

With the power of Xcode, the ease of Swift, and the revolutionary features of cutting-edge Apple technologies, students have the freedom to create the most innovative apps ever. Xcode includes everything students need to create amazing apps for all Apple platforms. The source code editor lets you transform or refactor code more easily, see source control changes alongside the related line, and quickly get details on upstream code differences.

Programming Laboratory

Programming laboratory is equipped with high configuration computer systems to support programming languages. Students learn practical aspects of diversity of concepts in programming languages like C and C++ which are basic blocks of computer engineering. In order to acquire advanced knowledge towards building high-end software students learn programming languages like Java, Advanced Java,NET, Advanced .Net and Android.

Network Laboratory

Network laboratory is equipped with high configuration computer systems to holds networking. Students acquire practical knowledge of the diversity of concepts in subjects like Computer Networks, Cryptography & Network Security and Advance Computer Networks. Students learn how computers communicate in networks, learn different protocols of networking and how to design secure system over network.
Project Laboratory

Project laboratory equipped with high configuration computer systems having a high-speed internet connection to accomplish project work. The laboratory is open after college hours for students to complete their projects in leisure time. Laboratory is prepared with different softwares to support project implementation as well as project documentation.
