MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical engineering is the study of physical machines that may involve force and movement. It is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science, to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems

The aim of the mechanical engineering is to create a flexible undergraduate educational experience in

  • design
  • mathematics modeling
  • computing
  • management
  • engineering science
  • humanities and social sciences
. The students completing their B.Tech course are fully prepared for technological career in industry as well in further post-graduate or management courses. The students can also utilize these interdisciplinary programs as an initiation for professional careers in business or public service sectors.The department organizes corporate training programs, besides undertaking industrial consultancy. This imparts immense benefit to students by way of good placements, well-equipped computational laboratories with required software and industrial exposure.

Labs and Computing Description
Engineering Drawing

Designing plays a major where information can be conveyed by just by drawings. It is a central facility common to all first year B.Tech students in School of Engineering. Each Drafting lab is well furnished. This lab develops and gives hands on experience on introduction to engineering graphics and drafting tools, geometric construction, dimensions, hatching and text along with conic sections, special curves and orthographic projection.
CAD lab

The CAD laboratory in university is a central facility available with the school of Mechanical Engineering, wherein all the section of students from the School (UG & PG), research scholars and faculty can work with 2-D and 3-D design and analysis packages. Students are encouraged to learn, practice and apply the knowledge gained for the design point of view.
Dynamics of Machine Lab

The objective of this lab is to impart practical knowledge on design and analysis of mechanisms in the machine tools. Dynamics of machine lab offers the students in conducting the experiments related to theory subjects like kinematics and dynamics of machines.
Thermal lab

Thermal science is a major part of the mechanical engineering where students made to familiarize the basic concepts of thermal sciences. From the knowledge gained by the other courses like thermal engineering and automobile engineering students shall understand the principle in propelling the vehicle.
